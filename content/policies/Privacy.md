## **1\. Introduction**

We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our tutors, students, and volunteers. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information in compliance with the UK GDPR and other applicable data protection laws.

## **2\. Who We Are**

Give and Grow is a fully remote tutoring agency providing online educational services to students. We act as the data controller for the personal data we collect.

## **3\. What Data We Collect**

We may collect the following types of personal data:

* **Students & Guardians**: Name, contact details, academic information, session recordings (if consented), feedback  
* **Tutors**: Name, contact details, qualifications, DBS checks, payment information  
* **Website Visitors**: IP address, browser type, cookies, usage data 

## **4\. How We Use Your Data**

We use personal data to:

* Deliver tutoring services  
* Match students with appropriate tutors  
* Process payments and invoices  
* Monitor quality and safeguarding  
* Communicate updates and service changes  
* Improve our platform and services

## **5\. Sharing Your Data**

We do not sell personal data. We may share it with:

* Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)  
* Legal authorities if required by law

## **7\. Data Retention**

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

* Student records: up to 1 years after last session  
* Tutor records: up to 6 years for tax and legal compliance  
* Session recordings: up to 12 months (if applicable)

## **8\.  Your Rights**

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

* Access your data  
* Correct inaccuracies  
* Request deletion  
* Object to processing  
* Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **give.and.grow.cic@gmail.com**

## **9\. Cookies**

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

## **10\.  Security**

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect data, including:

* Encrypted communications  
* Secure login credentials  
* Regular audits and access controls
